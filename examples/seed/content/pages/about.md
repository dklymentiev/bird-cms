This is a Bird CMS demo site. Three articles, two pages, three categories - installed automatically by the install wizard so the site looks like something the moment you finish setup.

Replace this page with your own copy. The file lives at `content/pages/about.md`. Edit it, delete it, or move it - Bird picks up the change on the next request.

## What goes here

A typical "About" page covers a few things:

- **Who you are** - one or two sentences about you or the team
- **What this site is for** - the editorial scope, the audience, what readers should expect
- **How to reach you** - email, social links, mailing list

Keep it short. Most visitors land here from a footer link, scan for the contact info, and leave. Make that easy.
